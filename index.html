Phase 1 & 2: Analysis & Diagnosis

NotebookLM

Grounding & Trend Analysis

Use these prompts after uploading your CSV to identify the core story within your data.

"Analyse the attached tracking sheet. Identify the top 3 concepts mastered and the top 2 where a significant performance gap exists across the class. Which students show a high pre-test score but struggled with the final assessment?"
"Based on the results, identify which specific topics have the widest gap in class performance. Which students scored highly overall but struggled with one specific sub-topic (e.g. Mixed Fractions)?"
NotebookLM

Misconception Deep-Dive

Focusing on specific question data to understand why students are struggling.

"Look at Question [X] and [Y]. These had the lowest averages. What is the likely misconception holding these students back? Provide a summary of the 'gap' for each identified group of students."

Phase 3 & 4: Differentiation & Resources

Gemini

Strategic Grouping

Move the insights from NotebookLM into Gemini to organize your classroom.

"I have these student groups based on their struggle with [Topic]. Group the class into three tiers: Tier 1: Needs immediate reteach on foundational concepts. Tier 2: Needs fluency practice with scaffolds. Tier 3: Ready for non-routine problem solving. Provide a suggested seating or intervention plan."
Gemini

Resource Synthesis

Generating the actual materials for the lesson.

"Create a 10-minute reteach activity for the group that struggled with [Specific Topic], including a visual analogy, 3 check-for-understanding questions, and a 'Challenge' extension for those who finish early."
"Generate a 'Success Criteria' checklist for [Topic] that is broken down into 4 simple, illustrated steps for my Tier 1 group."
